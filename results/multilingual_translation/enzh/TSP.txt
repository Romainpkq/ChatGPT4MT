This new environment has different resources and competitors, so the new population requires different characteristics or adaptations to be a strong competitor than what they needed before.
The original population has not changed at all. It still needs the same adaptations as before.
Over time, the new population begins to adapt to its new environment, and then it becomes less and less similar to the other population.
